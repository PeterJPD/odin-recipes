Strogonoff Recipe

Description

The strogonoff, despite being a very famous recipe among brazilians, was born in Rurssia and whas a noble dish.

In this recipe you will learn to prepare the Chicken Strogonoff, but there are many variations of this dish out there.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts cut into cubes
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/3 cup of mustard
  • 1 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • Potato Chips
    •

    Steps

    1. In a pan, mix the chicken, garlic, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.
    2. In a large skillet, melt the butter and brown the onion.
    3. Add the seasoned chicken until golden.
    4. Add the mushrooms, ketchup and mustard.
    5. Add the cream and remove from the heat before boiling.
    6. Serve with white rice and potatoes chips.
