Feijoada Recipe

Description

Feijoada is a typical dish from Rio de Janeiro and is widely consumed throughout Brazil. Despite being a dish with many variations, the typical complete feijoada is the one that takes all the salted meats and is served with slices of orange.

As it is considered a heavy dish, there are those who prefer to make feijoada simple: for this, you can bet on using only sausage, bacon and jerky, making your feijoada dish lighter without losing flavor.

    Steps

    1. Soak the meats for 36 hours or more, change the water several times, if it is a hot or summer environment, put ice on top or in cold layers.
    2. Put to cook step by step: the hard meats, then the soft meats.
    3. When it's soft, put the beans, and remove the meat.
    4. Finally, season the beans.
