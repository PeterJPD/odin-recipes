Pudim Recipe

Description

The pudding and flan are similar desserts, but different from each other! In addition to the historical and origin difference - the pudding is Portuguese and the flan is French - they have a difference in the method of preparation and ingredients.

The pudding, for example, takes condensed milk, while the flan does not, having its base of eggs and vanilla essence.

Ingredients

Syrup

  • 1 cup (tea) of sugar
    •

    Pudim

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 2 cans of milk (use the condensed milk can as a measure)
  • 3 eggs
    •

    Steps

    Syrup

    1. In a wide-bottomed pan, melt the sugar until golden.
    2. Add 1/2 cup (tea) of hot water and stir with a spoon.
    3. Let it boil until the sugar clumps dissolve and the syrup thickens.
    4. Line a mold with a central hole (19 cm in diameter) with the syrup and set aside.

    Pudim

    1. Mix all the pudding ingredients in the blender and pour into the reserved form.
    2. Bake in a bain-marie, in a medium oven (180ยบ C), for about 1 hour and 30 minutes.
    3. Once cooled, refrigerate for about 6 hours.
    4. Unmold and serve next.
